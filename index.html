ReachNow Machine Learning Student Engineer • Jan 2018 - Jun 2018 Seattle, WA ReachNow is a Mobility Service Company by BMW. Users can use their smartphones to reserve and pick up cars on the streets of Seattle. They can choose to keep the cars for as long as they would like. After finishing the trips, users can drop the car off at any legal street parking spots in Seattle At ReachNow, I designed a Machine Learning (ML) model to predict cars demand in Seattle area during a day based on various parameters such as: day of the week, month, hour, weather, public transportation, etc. This ML Model is aimed to combat the mismatch in demand and supply during rush hours that ReachNow is facing. With the predictions provided by the model, ReachNow can administrate car relocations to the more popular areas. Final Poster for this Project
